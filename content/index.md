Text CAD
==========================

> Test CAD for your hobby projects.

> It allows you to create simple STL 3D models, save them, export for 3D printing.

> You can do assemblies from a number of 3D objects.

> You can also generate g-code for simple milling.

> You can draw your stuff by typing text only!
